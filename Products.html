Temperament: Playful, Docile, Fearless, Affectionate

RABBITS
~~~~~~~~~

Rabbits are small mammals in the family Leporidae of the order Lagomorpha. Oryctolagus cuniculus includes the European rabbit species and its descendants, the world's 305 breeds of domestic rabbit.

Lifespan: 1 – 2 years (In the wild)
Daily sleep: 8.4 hours (Adult, In captivity)
Phylum: Chordata
Gestation period: Eastern cottontail: 27 days
Mass: Eastern cottontail: 1.2 kg, Desert cottontail: 880 g
Length: Eastern cottontail: 37 cm, Desert cottontail: 33 cm

TORTOISES
~~~~~~~~~~~

Tortoises are a family, Testudinidae. Testudinidae is a Family under the order Testudines and suborder Cryptodira. There are fourteen extant families of the order Testudines, an order of reptile commonly known as turtles, tortoises, and terrapins.

Scientific name: Testudinidae
Kingdom: Animalia
Class: Reptilia
Family: Testudinidae; Batsch, 1788
Mass: African spurred tortoise: 32 – 45 kg, Gopher tortoise: 5.5 kg,
Speckled cape tortoise: 95 – 160 g

LOVE BIRDS
~~~~~~~~~~~~

A lovebird is the common name of Agapornis, a small genus of parrot. Eight species are native to the African continent, with the grey-headed lovebird being native to Madagascar.

Lifespan: 10 – 15 years
Clutch size: 4 – 6
Scientific name: Agapornis
Kingdom: Animalia
Mass: Rosy-faced lovebird: 55 g, Fischer's lovebird: 48 g
